Current Research Projects



My research involves using novel isotopic geochemical techniques to study the provenance and weathering signatures of marine sediments. I study the radiogenic isotopes of neodymium, strontium and lead in sediment and used these as a proxy to support a contribution of detrital sediment during Meltwater Pulse 1a to the Mississippi river.

In addition I'll be looking into North American permafrost records utilizing speleothem growth in Karst cave systems.
